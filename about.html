About Woxsen University

Woxsen University is a premier institution committed to redefining education through innovation and excellence. Known for its cutting-edge infrastructure and interdisciplinary approach, Woxsen fosters a culture of creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration.

With a strong focus on entrepreneurship, technology, and global outlook, the university provides students with unparalleled opportunities to excel in a rapidly evolving world. Its incubation centers and industry partnerships make it a hub for innovation and transformative learning.

Woxsen University has a fully residential campus of around 200 acres, accommodating over 5,000 students. The university is situated on the Mumbai-Hyderabad Highway NH65, around 30 km from Sangareddy and 75 km from Hyderabad.

About the Centre of Talent Development (COTD)

The Centre of Talent Development (COTD) at Woxsen University is a dynamic hub dedicated to empowering students with essential life skills and bridging the gap between academic excellence and industry readiness. Aligned with Woxsen's mission to shape future-ready leaders, COTD equips students with vital skills in communication, problem-solving, and teamwork.

Through innovative events like Innoverse 2.0, leadership series with industry trailblazers, thought-provoking book launches, and engaging Olympiads, COTD fosters creativity, collaboration, and critical thinking amongst students, preparing them for a competitive professional world.

Recap of Previous INNOVERSE

The previous INNOVERSE, held on September 27 and 28, was an intense internal university competition that brought together over 100 participants across 25 teams. The 12-hour hackathon was a platform for tech enthusiasts, innovators, and problem solvers to showcase their creativity.

Participants engaged in a night of rigorous brainstorming, coding, and pitching, working tirelessly to craft solutions to real-world challenges. The event demonstrated remarkable innovation and teamwork, with an infectious energy that filled the room.

The final presentations to the jury were awe-inspiring, featuring a diverse range of ideas from cutting-edge prototypes to impactful community solutions. The event concluded with a grand prize distribution ceremony, awarding a 50K prize pool to the top teams.
